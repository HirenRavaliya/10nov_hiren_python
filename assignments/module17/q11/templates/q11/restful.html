REST Principles Applied
  • ✅ Nouns in URLs (/doctors/, not /get-doctors/)
  • ✅ HTTP verbs for actions (GET, POST, PUT, DELETE)
  • ✅ Status codes (200, 201, 404, 400)
  • ✅ Stateless (no session state)
  • ✅ JSON responses
  • ✅ Resource-based routing
